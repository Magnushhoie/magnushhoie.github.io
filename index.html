About

I am an PhD student in immuno-informatics at the Technical University of Denmark. I am excited about applications of machine learning for design and optimization of antibody therapeutics. Previously, I have contsructed machine-learning algorithms for prediction of epitope binding, protein structure prediction and TCR-p-MHC binding. In my current work at the Oxford Protein Informatics group, we are developing antibody language models for developability and mutational tolerance prediction.
